Explore your imagination

Cozumel Sunsets

Cozumel is known for its beautiful and breath taking sunsets. Slow down and enjoy the end of your daily adventure with a sunset eveining.

Punta Sur Ecological Park

Punta Sur Eco Beach is our number one recommended spot to see! It is said to be the best beach on the island, located on the South shore-it covers 2,500 acres. It is home to exotic birds, crocodiles, sea turtles, flowers and beautiful beaches. This is an absolute must see! It is a perfect spot for couples renting our scooters, or groups renting our jeeps!

Faro Celarain Lighthouse

The Faro Celarain Lighthouse is another must see within the Punt Sur Eco Park. Constructed to warn sailors at night, it is now part of a maritime museum. It is 127 steps to the top to get 360Â° views of the Island, on a clear day you can even see the coast of Cuba! Make sure to check the weather on our home page

Snorkeling
